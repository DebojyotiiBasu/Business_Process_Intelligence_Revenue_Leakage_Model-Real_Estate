# Business_Process_Intelligence_Revenue_Leakage_Model-Real_Estate
Real Estate Revenue Leakage &amp; Operational Intelligence system quantifying payment delays, churn risk, pricing gaps, and maintenance inefficiencies. Demonstrates 51% projected cash cycle improvement and ₹5.9L monthly liquidity acceleration through structured process redesign.
Business Process Intelligence & Revenue Leakage Detection System for Real Estate Operations
An end-to-end operational analytics model designed to identify revenue leakage, working capital inefficiencies, lease-cycle risk exposure, and maintenance cost optimization opportunities.
The system integrates collection delay modeling, pricing dispersion analysis, renewal risk segmentation, escalation enforcement audit, and vendor efficiency benchmarking to simulate strategic process redesign impact.
Key outcomes include:
•	51% projected improvement in cash realization cycle
•	₹5.9 Lakhs monthly working capital acceleration
•	13.17% pricing dispersion exposure identified
•	33% churn risk and 88% renewal exposure quantified
Built using Excel-based data modeling, pivot analytics, risk bucketing, and financial simulation techniq
